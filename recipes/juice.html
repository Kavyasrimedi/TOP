ORANGE JUICE

DESCRIPTION

Fresh orange juice is a refreshing and healthy drink, perfect for any time of the dayâ€”especially summer mornings or post-workout hydration.

It's packed with vitamin C and natural sweetness, making it a delicious and energizing alternative to packaged juices.

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 large fresh oranges
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or sugar (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  • Mint leaves for garnish (optional)

STEPS

  1. Wash and cut: Wash the oranges thoroughly. Cut them in half.
  2. Juicing: Squeeze the oranges using a manual or electric juicer. Strain if you prefer pulp-free juice.
  3. Add lemon juice and honey or sugar if desired. Mix well.
  4. Pour into a glass over ice cubes for a chilled drink.
  5. Garnish with mint leaves and serve immediately.
