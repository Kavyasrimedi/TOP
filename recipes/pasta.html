RED SAUCE PASTA

DESCRIPTION

This easy pasta recipe is perfect for a quick lunch or dinner. It's light, flavorful, and pairs well with garlic bread or a fresh green salad.

Pasta dishes are versatile and simple to prepare, making them a great choice for busy weekdays or relaxed weekends.

INGREDIENTS

  • 200g pasta (spaghetti or penne)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes (optional)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

STEPS

  1. Cook pasta: Boil the pasta in salted water according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare sauce: In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and chili flakes, sauté for a minute.
  3. Add cherry tomatoes and cook until they soften, about 3–4 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and toss everything together.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil and grated Parmesan if desired.
